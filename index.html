About Us

The main purpose of your About Us page is to give visitors a glimpse into the identity of either a person or business. As users discover your brand, they need to distinguish what sets you apart and makes you… you. This often requires finding the right balance between compelling content and a design carefully planned to look the part. Conveying your identity in a fun and approachable – but also reliable and informative – way is challenging. If you know who you are and your goal for your site, the About Us page should come naturally. However, if you’re looking for some inspiration, you can always check out these 25 examples of creative and engaging About Us pages.
